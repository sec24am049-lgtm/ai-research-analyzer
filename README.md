AI Research Analyzer

AI Research Analyzer is an intelligent platform that transforms complex research papers into clear, actionable insights. It leverages artificial intelligence and natural language processing to analyze academic content, identify key findings, and accelerate the research process.

Features

• Automated research paper analysis
• Key concept and insight extraction
• Intelligent content summarization
• Topic and trend identification
• User-friendly modern interface

Vision

To make research more accessible by helping students, researchers, and innovators understand academic knowledge faster and more effectively.

Technologies

• Artificial Intelligence
• Machine Learning
• Natural Language Processing
• React
• Python
